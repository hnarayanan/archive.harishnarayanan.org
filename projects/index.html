Being a geek, it’s sometimes hard to separate the work I do for my day job as a mathematical physics researcher and the projects I do for fun. But I am going to try, and what follows is an incomplete list of my personal projects, broadly classfied as web sites, software projects and mechanics education. Many of these projects are open source, so feel free to try them out and do let me know if you’re interested in contributing to them!
