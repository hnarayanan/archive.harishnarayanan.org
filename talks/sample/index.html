As a participant of a workshop on Communicating Scientific Research (conducted by Prof. Michael Alley and Melissa Marshall from Pennsylvania State University, March & May 2009), I had the opportunity to have a talk of mine critiqued. Going through the process was personally beneficial to me, and I came away with the following recording of the talk that I gave. The recording also contains critique from the audience, and I am putting it up here in the hope that it will be of interest or help to others.

This talk was on an adaptive algorithm suitable for biomedical flows, and was geared toward a general scientific audience.
